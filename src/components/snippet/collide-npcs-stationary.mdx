#### Case: B is stationary.

If the over-approximating rectangles `npcA.getWalkBounds()` and `npcB.anim.staticBounds` don't intersect,
the NPCs won't intersect either. <Icon icon="qed-icon" inline large bottom />

Otherwise $A$ has position:
- $p_A(t) := {\tt seg_A.src} + t . s_A . {\tt seg_A.tangent}$, where:
- $seg_A := {\tt \{ src: npcA.getPosition(), dst: npc.getTarget(), tangent: \widehat{dst - src} \} }$
  <SideNote>The hat ^ means <i>take the unit vector</i>.</SideNote>

We seek a bounded time $t$ such that $|p_A(t) - p_B|^2 \leq dist_{\rm min}^2$
where $p_B := {\tt npcB.getPosition()}$ is fixed.

$i_{AB} := {\tt seg_A.src} - {\tt npc_B.getPosition()}$

- $a := s_A^2$
- $b := 2.s_A . dp_A$
- $c := dist_{AB}^2 - dist_{\rm min}^2$
